📚 How to Use the App
Your step-by-step guide to your Marmaroph sanctuary.
Getting Started
1
Log in from the website
Go to marmaroph.com and log into your account. You'll be redirected to this members app automatically.
2
Choose a ritual
Browse the available rituals in Home or My Rituals. Tap any locked ritual to learn more and subscribe. Not sure which one? Use Choose Your Next Ritual to take the quiz.
3
Subscribe and pick your start month
Each ritual starts on the 1st of the month. Choose the month you'd like to begin and complete your subscription ($29/month).
4
Begin your ritual
Once subscribed, your ritual card will say CURRENT. Tap "Begin Ritual" (or "Continue Ritual" if you've already started) to open your daily practice.
Inside Your Ritual (e.g. Mindful Eating)
🌱
Welcome page
Read the introduction to understand the philosophy behind the ritual. Scroll down to learn the science and meet the experts. Then tap "Begin Today's Ritual".
📅
Daily home screen
This is your daily hub. You'll see your current day (e.g. Day 3 of 30), today's lesson, and a button to start the day's practice. Each day has a new lesson, reflection, or activity.
✎
Daily lessons
Read through the day's content at your own pace. Some days include interactive tools (like the Ritual Space Analyser or the Fridge Check) and journal prompts for reflection. Complete each lesson to unlock the next day.
🌟
Completing a day
After finishing the lesson, tap "Complete Day" at the bottom. Your progress is saved and the next day's content becomes available. You can revisit past days anytime.
🛠
Tools and resources
Some rituals include special tools you can use anytime. For Mindful Eating, you'll find the Ritual Space Analyser, Fridge Check, and Habit Tracker in the tools section.
Install the App on Your Phone
📱
iPhone (Safari)
1. Open app.marmaroph.com in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
5. Open the app from your home screen — it will log you in automatically
🤖
Android (Chrome)
1. Open app.marmaroph.com in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots) at the top right
3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
4. Open the app from your home screen
Tips
✦Open the app at the same time each day to build your ritual habit.
✦You don't need to finish the entire lesson in one sitting — your progress is saved.
✦Use the journal prompts — writing makes the practice stick.
✦After completing a ritual, you can choose a new one from the Rituals page.
✦Need help? Reach out through the Community section or email us.
